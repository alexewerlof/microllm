# On-call Fatigue and Burnout

On-call fatigue is a real problem in SRE. It occurs when SREs are overwhelmed by the number of alerts.

## Symptoms

- **Burnout**: Feeling overwhelmed and exhausted.
- **Reduced Productivity**: Difficulty focusing.

## Prevention

- **Adequate Support**: Providing SREs with the support they need.
- **Rotation Schedules**: Creating fair and balanced schedules.
- **Toil Reduction**: Automating manual tasks.
